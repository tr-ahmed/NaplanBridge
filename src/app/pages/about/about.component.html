Our Story

NAPLAN Bridge was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform the way students prepare for their NAPLAN assessments. Our founders, experienced educators and technologists, recognized the need for a comprehensive, engaging, and accessible online learning platform.

What started as a small initiative to help a handful of students has grown into a thriving community of thousands of learners across Australia. We've continuously evolved our platform, incorporating feedback from students, parents, and educators to create the most effective learning experience possible.

Today, NAPLAN Bridge stands as a trusted partner in education, helping students build confidence, master essential skills, and achieve their academic goals. Our commitment to innovation and excellence continues to drive us forward as we expand our offerings and reach more students every day.
