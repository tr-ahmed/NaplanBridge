1. Introduction

NAPLAN Bridge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform designed for Australian students preparing for NAPLAN assessments.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth
  • School year level (Year 3, 5, 7, or 9)
  • Parent/guardian contact information (for students under 18)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

2.2 Educational Data

  • Assessment results and performance metrics
  • Learning progress and activity logs
  • Practice test scores
  • Study time and engagement statistics

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our educational services
  • Personalize learning experiences and content recommendations
  • Track student progress and generate performance reports
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send important updates about NAPLAN assessments and platform features
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations under Australian law

4. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988

5. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for students in Years 3, 5, 7, and 9. For users under 18 years of age:

  • Parent/guardian consent is required for account creation
  • We collect only necessary information for educational purposes
  • Parents have the right to access, modify, or delete their child's data
  • We do not sell or share children's data with third parties for marketing

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Educational Institutions: With your consent, we may share progress reports with schools
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in platform operations (payment processors, hosting providers)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Australian law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Parents/Guardians: Access to their child's educational data and progress

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Your Rights Under Australian Law

Under the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze usage patterns, and maintain login sessions. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Educational data is retained to track long-term progress. You may request deletion at any time, subject to legal and operational requirements.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed within Australia. If data is transferred internationally, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with Australian privacy laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through a prominent notice on our platform. Continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
